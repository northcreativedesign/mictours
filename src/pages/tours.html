VISIT METLAKATLA

Once you arrive in Ketchikan, Alaska- Metlakatla is just a short boatride away.

If you are a cruiseship passenger, you can book a tour on the ship. Tour can also be booked directly through Allen Marine Tours, as well as using this site to find more information about each tour.

If you are looking at a longer venture in Southeast Alaska, you can reach Annette Islands Reserves by air or by sea. Alaska Marine Highyway System has ferry sevices Thursday through Monday with two daily round trips, while flight services such as Pacific Airways or Taquan Air, make several flights per day.
